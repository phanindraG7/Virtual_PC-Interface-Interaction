1. Gestures for Volume Control :

  • Show your hand towards the camera and then slowly move your thumb (finger 1).
  • Move finger 2 (index finger) towards or away from finger 1 to adjust the volume (closer to decrease, farther to increase).
  • For precise volume control, fold finger 5 (pinky finger).

2. Explore Hand Gestures for PowerPoint Control :

  • Click "Run Presentation" and upload a specific PowerPoint file.
  • Raise finger 5 (pinky finger) to move to the next slide, and raise finger 1 (thumb) for the previous slide.
  • Raise finger 2 and finger 3 to display a pointer.
  • For drawing on the slide, use finger 2 (index finger).
  • To undo a drawing, simultaneously show three fingers 2, 3, and 4 simultaneously.

3. Virtual Mouse Gestures for Navigation :

  • Point your finger 2 (index finger) to move the cursor in the displayed direction.
  • Touch your thumb and index finger together for a double-click operation.
